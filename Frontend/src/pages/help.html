Help & Support

We're here to help you get the most out of your Smart Warehouse system. Find answers to common questions or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in to the system?

Use your registered email and password to log in. If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I update the inventory data?

Navigate to the relevant category page (Grains, Vegetables, Fruits, or Seeds) and click the "View Details" button. On the details page, use the update functionality to modify quantities. All changes are logged for accountability.

How can I access historical inventory data?

Go to the Dashboard section and use the date range selector to view historical data. You can export reports in various formats including PDF, CSV, and Excel.

How do I set up alerts for low inventory?

Navigate to the Alerts section from the main menu. Select "New Alert" and configure the threshold levels for each product category. You can receive notifications via email, SMS, or within the app.

How do I add new users to the system?

Admin users can add new users by going to the Settings page and selecting "User Management". Click "Add New User" and fill in the required details. You can set different permission levels based on user roles.
